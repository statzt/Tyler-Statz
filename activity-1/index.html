What is basketball

Basketball: sport where you have to put a basketball in a hoop. You have to shoot at the hoop to score some points. To move in basketball you have to dribble the ball while walking towards the hoop.

Things You Need in Basketball.

  • Basketball Shoes
  • Basketball or any ball
  • A Hoop
  • Court

Where to watch basketball

You can watch the games on TV. The stations to watch the games on usually on ESPN, TNT or the NBA station if you can get it. You can also watch the games on the ESPN app if you have the app.
